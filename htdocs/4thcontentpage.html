HOW TO SUPPORT THE LGBT COMMUNITY

Be an Ally! Support the LGBTQIA+ Community!

There are number of ways to support the movement:

  1. Educate yourself on issues regarding the LGBT Community
  2. Speak out against homophobia and LGBT discriminations and harassment
  3. Do not say anti-gay slurs and call out people who use them
  4. Coming out is a very hard thing for the LGBT Community. Make sure to support and give them love if they do so.
  5. Do not out someone else's sexuality or gender identity without permission.
  6. Donate to LGBT organizations
